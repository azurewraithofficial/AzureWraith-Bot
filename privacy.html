Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Azure Wraith ("we," "our," or "the Bot") is committed to protecting the privacy of our community members. This policy explains what data we use to provide our services.

1. Data Collection

To provide utility and tracking features, the Bot processes the following Discord data:

  • User Identifiers: Discord User IDs are stored to track invites and community activity.
  • Server Data: Server IDs and channel IDs are stored to save your custom panel settings.
  • Invite Data: We monitor invite codes to provide "who invited who" statistics.

2. Data Usage

Data is used exclusively for:

  • Operating modern utility panels and buttons.
  • Tracking invite metrics for server growth.
  • Automating community roles and moderation.

3. Data Security & Deletion

We do not sell or share user data with third parties. Data is stored securely and is deleted automatically if the Bot is kicked from a server. You may request manual data removal by contacting the owner, Azure Wraith, via our support channels.
