01 / ACQ
Data Acquisition
What we observe and record during bot operation
Snowflake Identifiers
Discord User, Server, and Channel IDs are stored to anchor settings and configurations to your specific environment — nothing more.
Interaction Metadata
Command usage logs are retained temporarily to optimize performance and resolve bugs. These logs are non-persistent by design.
Invite Matrix Data
Vanity and standard invite usage patterns are monitored and stored exclusively to power our invite tracking feature suite.
Content Filtering
Automated systems scan message hashes — not raw content — to detect and neutralize spam and scam outbreaks across the Void.
02 / USE
Data Utilization
How collected data is put to work
Infrastructure Management
Powering the modern UI panels, utility dashboards, and control surfaces that make Azure Wraith's interface feel seamless.
Community Progression
Activity weights are calculated from interaction data to fairly award roles, permissions, and progression milestones.
Security Enforcement
Heuristic moderation algorithms run continuously to maintain a safe Void — detecting anomalous patterns before they escalate.
03 / RIGHTS
Data Sovereignty
Your rights over the data we hold

We operate under a strict ZERO-PERSISTENCE policy for all non-essential data. This means that the moment Azure Wraith is removed from your server, every piece of associated guild data is immediately flagged for permanent deletion from our active buffers — no archives, no shadows, no remnants.


You retain the right to request data review or deletion at any time, even while the bot remains active in your server. Reach out via the official Community Server and the Lead Architect will respond to your inquiry.

Data Integrity Inquiries

Have questions about how your data is handled? The Lead Architect is available through the official Azure Wraith Community Server.
